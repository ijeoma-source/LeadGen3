My Approach

How I Approach Lead Generation

01

Understand the Buyer

Before building any list, I study the product, the market, and the competition to map exactly who the right prospect is, demographically and psychographically, and why they would be a fit.

02

Build With Precision

Using LinkedIn, Apollo, Clay, Hunter.io, Meta platforms, online communities, and custom scraping, I build lists that are enriched, vetted, and annotated with reasoning for every contact included.

03

Enable the Close

A good list is only half the job. I also develop the outreach infrastructure and discovery frameworks that give the sales team the best chance of converting a conversation into a deal.

I have spent years in early-stage environments not observing from the outside, but doing the actual work of figuring out who to talk to and how to reach them.
